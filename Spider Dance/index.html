Spider Dance

This track plays as you fight Miss Muffet in the Hot Lands in Undertale. It's the 59th track in the soundtrack. While its BPM is 115 and the key it is played in is F minor with a time signature of 2, what makes this track interesting is its leitmotif it contains. The leitmotif is Ghost Fight which is also a name of another track (this is somewhat of a trend with the other leitmotifs).

Ghost Fight leitmotif

All the songs that have this leitmotif are:

  • "Ghost Fight":
  • "Dummy!":
  • "Pathetic House":
  • "Spider Dance":
  • "Mad Mew Mew":
This leitmotif occurs with characters or places associated with ghosts, spiders, or both. It also connects some of the main area together. With Ghost Fight playing in the Ruins, Mad Mew Mew playing in the Snowdin, Pathetic House and Dummy! playing in the Waterfall, and Spider Dance playing in the Hotlands. It is truly interesting how a simple leitmotif can connect different things together. It's also noted that all of the tracks but one has a connection with the character Napstablook, the one track that doesn't have a connection with this character is Spider Dance.
